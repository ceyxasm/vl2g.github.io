At VL2G, we conduct weekly reading group sessions, where the graduate and undergraduate students take initiatives to present and discuss recent research papers in the areas of general group interest, which include Knowledge-aware Computer Vision, Multimodal query-guided Image Retrieval, Document Intelligence, Open-world Object Detection and Recognition and Visual Relationship Interpretation. These sessions promote a healthy discussion and exchange of ideas among the group members and are a great way to learn about the latest research in these areas.
